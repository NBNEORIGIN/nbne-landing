The website is what your customers see. The admin panel is where you run your business. Here’s what’s inside.

Dashboard

Today’s bookings, weekly revenue, staff on duty, pending actions — everything at a glance when you open the app.

📅

Booking Calendar

See all bookings by day, week, or staff member. Confirm, cancel, reschedule. Customers get instant email confirmations.

Stripe deposits, revenue reports, service breakdowns. See exactly what’s coming in and what each service earns.
