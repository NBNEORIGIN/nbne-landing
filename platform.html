The Platform

One system.
Everything you need.

Your website is just the front door. Behind it sits a complete business management platform. Here's what every module does — and why it matters.

📅
Bookings & Payments

Online Booking That Actually Works

Customers pick a service, choose their preferred staff member, select a time slot, and pay a deposit — all without picking up the phone. You see it in real time on your calendar. Double-booking is impossible.

Stripe handles the money. Deposits reduce no-shows. Customers get instant email confirmations. You get a clean calendar view of your day, your week, or your month.

  • Real-time availability — no double bookings
  • Stripe deposits and full payments
  • Staff assignment per booking
  • Day / week / month calendar views
  • Client history attached to every booking
  • Automatic email confirmations
👥
Staff Management

Your Team, Sorted

Every staff member gets a profile with their role, working hours, leave balance, and training records. Managers can see who's in, who's off, and who's overdue on training — all in one place.

Set weekly working hours per person. Track absences and approve leave requests. No more spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, or sticky notes on the fridge.

  • Staff profiles with roles and permissions
  • Weekly working hours configuration
  • Leave requests and approvals
  • Training records and expiry tracking
  • Shift patterns and rotas
  • Owner / Manager / Staff role hierarchy
⏱️
Timesheets

Know Exactly Who Worked What

Staff clock in via the app. Managers approve timesheets weekly. The system calculates hours automatically, factoring in breaks and overtime. No more guessing, no more arguments about hours.

Timesheets link directly to staff profiles and project codes, giving you clear visibility on labour costs per project, per person, or per week.

  • Digital clock-in / clock-out
  • Automatic hours calculation
  • Manager approval workflow
  • Project code allocation
  • Overtime and break tracking
  • Weekly and monthly summaries
📊
CRM & Lead Tracking

Never Lose a Lead Again

Every enquiry — whether it comes from your website, Instagram, a phone call, or a referral — gets tracked from first contact to paying customer. See your full pipeline at a glance and know exactly who needs following up.

Add notes, set follow-up dates, and track the estimated value of each lead. When a lead converts, their history is right there for the whole team to see.

  • Lead pipeline with drag-and-drop status
  • Source tracking (website, social, referral, manual)
  • Follow-up date reminders
  • Notes and activity history per lead
  • Estimated deal value tracking
  • Marketing consent management
🛡️
Compliance & Health & Safety

Stay Inspection-Ready, Every Day

Track every compliance requirement your business has — from fire risk assessments to PAT testing, food hygiene certificates to employer's liability insurance. The system tells you what's due, what's overdue, and what's missing.

When the inspector walks in, you're not scrambling through filing cabinets. Everything is digital, dated, and audit-trailed. Built for UK regulations.

  • Compliance register with status tracking
  • Expiry date alerts and reminders
  • Incident reporting with RIDDOR guidance
  • Risk assessment records
  • UK regulatory references built in
  • KPI dashboard for compliance health
📁
Document Vault

All Your Important Documents, One Place

Upload, categorise, and track every document your business needs — insurance certificates, policies, contracts, training certs, COSHH data sheets. The system knows what's expired and what's expiring soon.

We even pre-load the list of documents UK businesses are legally required to hold, so you know exactly what you're missing. Access levels mean staff only see what they're supposed to.

  • Categorised document storage
  • Expiry tracking with reminders
  • UK legal document checklist pre-loaded
  • Access levels: Owner / Manager / Staff
  • Tag-based organisation
  • One-click download
💬
Team Chat

Ditch the WhatsApp Group

A proper team messaging system built into the platform. Create channels for different topics — #general, #bookings, #urgent — and keep work chat separate from personal phones.

Messages are tied to the business, not personal numbers. When someone leaves, the history stays. When someone new joins, they can see the context. Simple, clean, and always available.

  • Channel-based team messaging
  • Real-time messages
  • Separate from personal phones
  • Message history preserved
  • Works on mobile and desktop
  • Integrated with the admin panel
